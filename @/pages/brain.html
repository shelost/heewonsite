BRAIN TEAM

Team spirit.

The IHS Brain Team is Ithaca's very own Quiz Bowl team, an amalgamation of experts on a truly random array of topics. The team did not have a logo (or any sort of branding, for that matter) when I joined, so - after some consulting with our coach - I created several versions of potential logos to be put up to an internal vote. The following designs are all the winners of their respective categories.

The Primary Logo

Our team's primary logo proudly features the classic red-and-gold Ithaca colors.

Trivia Night Poster

Spring Trivia Night is Ithaca High School's celebration of knowledge on delightfully obscure topics, hosted by our very own Brain Team. I designed the marketing for the 2020 edition of the event.

IFT Poster

IHS hosts a Quiz Bowl tournament on its very own grounds every November, and I wanted to make sure that 2019 was the first that could boast its very own branding.

The Social Media

The circular constraints of social media gave birth to this abbreviated version of our primary logo.
