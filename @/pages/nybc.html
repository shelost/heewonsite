NYBC Marketing

An Annual Treat.

Every year, four to five churches from the upper regions of New York gather to share three days of praise, worship, and communion during the New York Bible Conference (NYBC). Having been a regular attendee for over a decade by 2020, I was entrusted with the task of creating a) a poster for the main conference, b) a poster for the accompanying fundraising concert, and c) tickets for said concert.
