ITHACA PAINTBALL

The Battle Begins Here.

Ithaca Paintball is a locally owned business, a great place to host parties, gatherings, and more. I created a both new logo and business cards for the locale.

The Primary Logo

The "Paintball" text is crafted in the style of graffiti font, one of the most ubiquitous icons of the sport.

Business Card - Front

The front of the business card lists all contact information necessary to find Ithaca Paintball.

Business Card - Back

The back lists the potential circumstances that would lead one to seek out Ithaca Paintball.
